Robyn Rihanna Fenty, born in Saint Michael Parish, Barbados, is a singer, songwriter, actress, and businesswoman. Now 30 years old, she made her first hit song with Pon de Replay, in 2005. She has made many hits and branched off towards movies where she recently played one of the main characters in Ocean's Eight. She also started her makeup line, Fenty Beauty, where she had 40 diverse shades of foundations along with many highlighters. Her collection was a huge success, now setting the standards for other beauty brands. She has also collaborated with Puma to create slides, shoes, and a lingerie collection. Although Rihanna is only 30 years old, she has achieved a lot in her time in the industry.
